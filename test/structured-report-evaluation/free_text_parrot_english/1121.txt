Clinical Question: Fracture following a fall onto the hand  
Technique: X-ray of the left hand in two views (dorsopalmar and lateral).  
Findings: There is a displaced shaft fracture of the middle phalanx of the left ring finger. The fracture is oblique with dorsal and radial displacement. The joint surfaces are not involved. No additional fractures or dislocations in the left hand.  
Impression: Displaced shaft fracture of the middle phalanx of the left ring finger.
