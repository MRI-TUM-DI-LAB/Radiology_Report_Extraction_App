Clinical Information, Question, Justification for Procedure: Patient with cognitive deficits of unclear etiology, unsuccessful attempts at lumbar puncture due to spinal ossification, fluoroscopy-guided lumbar puncture requested. Anesthesia and Medications: Medications: Local anesthesia. Materials: Puncture needle: Quincke spinal needle (9 cm, 20G needle). Technique and Findings: Left lateral decubitus position. Identification of puncture site. Fluoroscopically guided puncture of the spinal canal at the L2/3 segment performed under sterile conditions without complications. Collection of cerebrospinal fluid totaling 5 x 3 ml.
