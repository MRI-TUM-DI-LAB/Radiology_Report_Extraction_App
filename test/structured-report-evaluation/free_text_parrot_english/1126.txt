Clinical Question: Fracture following sports injury?

Technique: X-ray of the right hand in two views (dorsopalmar and lateral) with a special view of the scaphoid.

Findings: The bony structures of the right hand appear normal. There is no evidence of fracture, dislocation, or bony destruction. The scaphoid is intact. Joint spaces are of normal wiRXh. Soft tissues are unremarkable.
