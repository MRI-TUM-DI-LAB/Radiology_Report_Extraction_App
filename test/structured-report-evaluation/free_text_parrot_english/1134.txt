Clinical Question: Fracture of the left ankle?  
Technique: X-ray of the left ankle in two views (anteroposterior and lateral).  
Findings: There is a fracture of the distal fibula with a step-off and posterior displacement. The fracture extends into the joint surface of the upper ankle joint. The medial malleolus is intact. The joint space of the upper ankle joint is obliterated. There is no evidence of talar dislocation. The bony structures of the foot appear unremarkable.  
Impression: Weber B fracture of the left ankle with displacement and intra-articular involvement.
